I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Algorithms, Data Structures and Foundations of Machine Learning Group at Aarhus University, hosted by Kasper Green Larsen. I completed my PhD at Northeastern University, where I was advised by Huy Le Nguyen and Jonathan Ullman. My research interests are in theoretical approaches to fairness in machine learning and data privacy.
