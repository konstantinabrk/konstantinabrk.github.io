I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Theory Group at Northeastern University, where I am advised by Huy Lê Nguyễn and Jonathan Ullman. My research interests are in theoretical approaches to fairness in machine learning and data privacy.

Before joining Northeastern, I received my diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens, where I completed my thesis under the guidance of Dimitris Fotakis.
